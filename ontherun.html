ON THE RUN

As you rot in your filthy prison cell beneath the fortress city of Brüttelburg, a shady jailor comes to you smelling of cheap gin & rancid oil offering an illicit deal. He has found something valuable that he needs to keep hidden from his cruel & fanatical commander, the High Magistrate. Unable to leave his post, the jailor offers you your freedom if you will retrieve & deliver a package to his crooked cousin in Pigskin Port. Will you accept this generous offer or refuse him & plot an escape on your own terms?
