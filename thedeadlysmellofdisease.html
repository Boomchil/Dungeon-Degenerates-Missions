The Deadly Smell of Disease

Seeping up through the cracks in the streets & festering in the refuse piles & stinking sewers of the Würstreich, the deadly smell of disease descends upon the Lowlands.

The dead pile up in the gutters & alleys, becoming filthy nests for flies. The panicked & overworked conscripts of the Plaguefinders & the Corpse Burners of the Holy Order desert their posts in droves; refusing to face the dangers of contagion. The Undercity Dungeon is flooded with new inmates; servants of the Empire who were caught shirking their duties, hiding in cellars, or fleeing to the North.

With their supply of expendable fodder dwindling, the leather-coated Plaguefinders are rounding up prisoners to replace those servants fallen to contagion & cowardice. If you will do the work of your more reluctant new cellmates, you can win a pardon & perhaps you might prove yourself a worthy servant of the Empire...
